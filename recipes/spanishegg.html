Spanish Fried Egg

Description

I don't even remember where I picked this up, but it's delicious. The crispy edges, which a lot of people say are terrible on eggs, actually hold craggles of flavor that I find irresistable. On top of rice, with tortillas, or on bread, you can't go wrong with this crispy breakfast (or any time really!) treat.

Ingredients

  • An egg
  • Cooking oil of your choosing
  • Seasoning
  • Carbohydrate of choice

Steps

  1. Heat a heavy pan (preferably cast iron) over high heat.
  2. Once heated, add enough oil to create a quarter to half-inch layer in the pan.
  3. Once oil is shimmering (or smoking), crack the egg directly into the pan. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Spoon oil over the top of the egg to cook the whites. You can choose to spoon oil on the yolk or not, depending on your preference.
  5. Remove from pan once top of egg is cooked. The bottom should separate smoothly, and be a crispy golden brown.
  6. Eat with your carb of choice, garnish with sauce if desired. I recommend rice, but toast, or tortillas and beans are good too!
